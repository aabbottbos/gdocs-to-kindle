Important: Approve Your Gmail Address

Amazon requires you to approve the email address that sends documents to your Kindle. Without this step, your documents won't arrive.

Steps to approve:

  1. Copy your Gmail address above
  2. Go to Amazon → Content & Devices → Preferences → Personal Document Settings
  3. Scroll to "Approved Personal Document E-mail List"
  4. Click "Add a new approved e-mail address"
  5. Paste your Gmail address and save
